Inspiration

Paying forward and giving back

Suspended Coffee

Having experienced first hand a suspended coffee, one of our developers was enthralled by the concept and hopes to scale this up and extend it to food for the needy. 

We all know markups for food can be pretty high, we hope to pair this up with the surge of food delivery services and provide a platform for users who would like to make their meals count.

Here at ForkTech we are commited to buying meals for the needy. This is done through a combination of partnerships with restaurants who can offer nutritious and tasty meals to be delivered to needy recipients after your scrumptious meal has been delivered to your home/office.

Our ideal world would be one where we can enjoy a nice michellin star quality meal while knowing that the same restaurant is preparing something in the same kitchen for a child who's parents are living paycheck to paycheck. Or an old retired lady who's struggling to make ends meet.
